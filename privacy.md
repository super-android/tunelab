# TuneLab Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 10, 2026
**App Version:** 1.0.0 and above

---

## 1. Introduction

TuneLab ("we," "our," or "the App") is a local-first tuning calculator for Forza Horizon 6. We are committed to protecting your privacy through a **Zero-Knowledge architecture** — the App is designed so that we are technically incapable of accessing your data, because we never receive it in the first place.

This policy explains how your data is handled when you use the App and what choices you have.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Storage

**No Server Collection.** TuneLab does not operate any central servers. We do not collect, store, process, or monitor your car selections, tuning values, session history, or personal identifiers of any kind.

**Local Storage Only.** All car data, calculated tunes, saved snapshots, ratings, and preferences are stored exclusively in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it yourself.

**Total Data Control.** You have complete control over all data stored by the App. Using the **"Full Reset"** feature within the App permanently and instantly erases all locally stored data from your device. No request to us is required — the data exists only on your device and you can delete it at any time.

---

## 3. Optional AI Services (Just-In-Time Consent)

TuneLab offers optional AI-assisted analysis through third-party providers. These features are entirely opt-in.

**Explicit Consent Required.** AI features are only activated if you deliberately choose to provide your own API key for one of the supported providers. No AI processing occurs without this affirmative action on your part.

**What is sent.** When you use an AI feature, your car's technical specifications and handling parameters are transmitted directly from your device to your chosen AI provider's API endpoint. This data:
- Does **not** pass through TuneLab infrastructure (we have none)
- Is **not** stored by TuneLab
- Is governed entirely by the privacy policy of the AI provider you selected

**API Key Storage.** Your API keys are stored locally on your device only. They are transmitted solely to the AI provider you selected and never to TuneLab or any other party.

**Withdrawing Consent.** You may remove your API key at any time via Settings → AI Provider → Clear Key. Once removed, no further data is sent to that provider by this App.

---

If you choose to use an AI provider, that provider acts as an independent Data Controller or Processor for the data you send them. Their processing is governed solely by their own privacy policies:

---

## 5. Your Rights Under GDPR and CCPA

**GDPR (European Users).** Because TuneLab stores no personal data on central infrastructure, the rights of access, rectification, portability, and erasure are fulfilled by your ability to manage your device's local storage directly and the Reset functions within the App. There is no data held by us to provide, correct, transfer, or delete on your behalf.

**CCPA (California Users).** TuneLab does not sell, share, or trade your personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. We have no personal information to sell.

**Data Minimisation.** In accordance with GDPR Article 5(1)(c), TuneLab is designed to collect the absolute minimum data necessary — in practice, zero data on our end.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

TuneLab is rated **Everyone** on the Google Play Store and does not target children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because all data remains on the user's device and we operate no collection infrastructure, no age-gating mechanism is technically necessary. If you believe a child has entered personal information into an AI provider through this App, please contact that provider directly per their policies.
