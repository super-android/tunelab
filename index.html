What it does
Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

TuneLab calculates a complete FH6 tune from your car's stats. Springs, damping, ARB, alignment, differential, gearing, tires — all of it.

⚡
Quick mode
Just PI, weight, and drivetrain. Full tune in under 30 seconds. No RPM data needed.
⚙
Full mode
Add RPM data, top speed, and tire sizes for a physics-based calculation including gear ratios and final drive.
✦
AI enhancement
Connect your free Gemini key. AI annotates every value with specific notes and flags issues. Your key, your data, your quota.
◎
Feel adjuster
Shift between stable and tail-happy, planted and aggressive. Real-time recalculation as you move the slider.
🔧
Tuning wizard
Tell the wizard what's wrong — understeer, snap oversteer, bouncy — and get specific fix suggestions.
📡
Live car database
500+ FH6 cars updated without an app update. New cars added to the list appear automatically on next launch.
